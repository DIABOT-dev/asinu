# Asinu — Family Health Companion

## App Name
Asinu: Family Health Companion

## Short Description (≤80 chars)
Keep your family in sync with daily health missions, streaks, and rewards.

## Full Description
Asinu motivates families to complete lightweight health missions every day. Track hydration, short walks, and mood check-ins, then celebrate progress together with points and badges. The app is a self-tracking companion and does **not** provide diagnosis or medical treatment.

### Key Features
- Mission Lite checklist: drink water, walk, mindful mood check-ins
- Shared family dashboard with rewards, badges, and donation goals
- Quick logs for meals, glucose, blood pressure, and more
- Invite relatives to follow each member’s streaks
- Reminder: Asinu only offers supportive tracking, not medical advice.
